How to run the interval sessions
Warm-up
10–15 min easy jog + 4–6 strides (80 m accelerations). Never skip — it's injury prevention.
The reps
Run each rep at the listed pace — controlled hard, not sprinting. Last rep should feel like the first.
Recovery (rec.)
Slow jog between reps, never stop dead. 800 m reps: ~90 sec jog. 1–2 km reps: ~4–5 min jog.
Cool-down
10 min easy jog after the last rep + 5 min light stretching. Helps clear lactate and speeds recovery.
Feel test
Rate 7–8 out of 10 effort. If the last rep is easy, go slightly faster next time. If you blow up, slow down.
